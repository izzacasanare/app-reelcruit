# Reelcruit — Product Sense

## What It Is

Reelcruit replaces first-round screening calls with AI-powered async video interviews. A hiring manager enters a job title, AI generates role-specific questions, candidates receive a unique link, record video answers on their own time, and the hiring manager reviews an AI-scored dashboard. They only speak to the top 3.

## Primary User

Hiring managers at MSPs and SMBs who open roles regularly (IT support, helpdesk, ops) and have no dedicated HR. They are time-poor, not recruiters, and currently spend 5–8 hours per role on first-round screening calls.

## Core Jobs-to-Be-Done

1. **Eliminate screening calls** — stop spending 5+ hours per role on first-round interviews
2. **Score and rank candidates automatically** — get an objective, consistent view of every candidate without bias creep
3. **Review on their own time** — watch 3-minute videos and read AI summaries instead of scheduling 20 calls

## Non-Negotiables

- Candidate video recording must work on mobile (no app install required)
- AI scoring must show reasoning — not just a number, a summary
- Completion rate must be trackable per job — if candidates aren't finishing, the hiring manager knows
- Unique interview links per candidate — not a shared URL anyone can access

## What This Is NOT

- Not a full ATS (applicant tracking system)
- Not a video conferencing tool (no live interviews)
- Not an enterprise HR suite
- Not a job board or sourcing tool
- Not a background check service
